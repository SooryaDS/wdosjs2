HIKKADUWA TURTLE HATCHERIES


The Sea Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Center, which aims to preserve some of the world's endangered turtle species, is located a few kilometers north of the main Hikkaduwa town. The southern shore of the island is home to several turtle hatcheries, including this one.

It began as a community initiative to help safeguard the southern coast's biodiversity, but it has since developed into a full-fledged hatchery and rescue facility with devoted personnel that toil around the clock to study, look after, and safeguard various turtle species. If you go to the Turtle Hatchery, there are loads of enjoyable activities. You can go to Hikkaduwa National Park, Hikkaduwa Beach, learn about turtles, go sightseeing, snap pictures, and much more.
